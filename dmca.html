DMCA Copyright Policy

1. No Hosting Policy

NTM Online respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies fully with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). We do not host, upload, or store any media files on our own servers.

2. Takedown Requests

If you are a copyright owner and believe that a link on our indexing site directs to your copyrighted work, we are committed to resolving the issue. Because we do not host the files, we cannot delete them from the internet, but we will promptly remove the indexing link from our database.

3. How to Contact Us

To submit a DMCA takedown request, please provide the exact URL(s) on NTM Online that link to the infringing material, along with proof of copyright ownership. You can submit these requests via our official Telegram channel, and they will be processed immediately.
