Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

NTM Online respects your privacy. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect personal identifying information (PII) such as names, emails, IP addresses, or physical locations.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage strictly to save your UI preferences (such as your choice between Light Mode and Dark Mode). This data never leaves your personal device.

3. Analytics

We utilize basic, anonymized Firebase analytics solely to maintain a public counter of total site visitors. This data cannot be traced back to individual users, contains no tracking cookies, and is never sold to third parties.
